Problem Definition

The business requires a clear and structured way to monitor biscuit sales performance across multiple cities, time periods, and customer segments. Revenue, expenses, and profit data exist but are not easily interpretable when spread across raw transaction records. There is a need to understand how different locations, payment methods, and customer demographics contribute to overall performance.

Core Objectives

  • To design a dynamic dashboard that integrates revenue, expense, profit, and quantity metrics.
  • Enable multi-dimensional analysis across locations, months, payment methods, and age groups.
  • Provide visibility into brand-level contribution and customer purchasing behavior.
  • Support quick comparisons of performance trends over time and across segments

Present-State Insight

The dashboard indicates strong overall revenue with a healthy profit margin relative to expenses. Certain cities show higher monthly market share, highlighting regional performance differences. Top biscuit brands contribute a significant portion of total sales, indicating brand concentration. Payment methods are fairly balanced, with a slight preference toward debit cards.

Future-State Strategy

Introduce forecasting models to anticipate demand across different cities and seasons. Enhance analysis by incorporating customer lifetime value and repeat purchase behavior. Integrate real-time sales tracking to improve responsiveness to market changes. Expand the dashboard to include inventory and supply chain insights for better planning. Incorporate market and product penetration analysis to assess growth headroom, adoption depth, and expansion opportunities across regions and product lines.

Decision Framework Mapping

  • Supports strategic planning by identifying high-performing regions and product segments.
  • Enables tactical adjustments through monthly and location-based performance tracking.
  • Improves operational efficiency by analyzing daily sales and growth fluctuations.
  • Helps align marketing and sales strategies with data-backed consumer behavior insights.

Data Credibility

The dashboard is built on structured transactional data ensuring consistency across metrics. Aggregations and percentage calculations are standardized for accurate comparisons. Interactive filters maintain transparency in how data is segmented and analyzed. Regular data updates and validation processes enhance reliability and user confidence.
