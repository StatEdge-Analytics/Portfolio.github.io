Problem Definition

The business needs a clear way to track cookie order fulfillment alongside sales performance across customers and regions. Order-level data exists but lacks a structured view to connect shipped quantities with revenue generation. There is a need to monitor how efficiently orders are being fulfilled over time while identifying key contributing customers and markets.

Core Objectives

  • To create an integrated dashboard combining total value, orders, shipped quantities, and customer insights.
  • Enable tracking of shipment performance trends over time for better operational visibility.
  • Provide detailed order-level breakdowns to support customer and revenue analysis.
  • Highlight country-wise contributions to understand geographic performance distribution.

Present-State Insight

The dashboard shows consistent fluctuations in shipment performance with noticeable peaks in certain periods. A small group of customers contributes significantly to total revenue and shipped quantities. The United States leads in total value, followed by other key international markets. Overall order fulfillment appears active, with steady shipment volumes across the timeline.

Future-State Strategy

Incorporate delivery time metrics to assess fulfillment efficiency and delays. Introduce predictive insights to forecast order volumes and shipping demand. Expand geographic analysis to include more granular regional performance. Integrate inventory tracking to align fulfillment capacity with demand patterns.

Decision Framework Mapping

  • Supports strategic decisions by identifying high-value markets and customers.
  • Enables tactical improvements through analysis of shipment trends and order patterns
  • Enhances operational control by linking fulfillment performance with sales outcomes.
  • Provides a data-driven foundation for optimizing logistics and customer service.

Data Credibility

DThe dashboard is built on detailed transactional and order-level data ensuring accuracy. Consistent aggregation methods maintain alignment between totals and breakdowns. Clear visibility of individual orders enhances transparency and traceability. Regular updates and validation processes strengthen reliability and user confidence.
