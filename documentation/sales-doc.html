Problem Definition

The organization often struggles to gain a unified view of its sales performance across products, customers, and regions. Key metrics such as sales, profit, and quantity are typically scattered across multiple sources, making analysis time-consuming and inefficient. There is a need for a centralized solution that clearly presents trends, top contributors, and regional performance in one place.

Core Objectives

  • To build an interactive dashboard that consolidates sales, profit, and quantity into a single visual interface.
  • Enable users to analyze performance by category, sub-category, customer, and geography.
  • Provide dynamic filtering by year, month, and category for granular insights.
  • Highlight top-performing customers and products to support strategic decisions.

Present-State Insight

The dashboard shows steady growth in yearly sales, with a peak in 2024. Technology leads in category sales, followed closely by Furniture and Office Supplies. Top customers contribute significantly, indicating a reliance on key accounts. Monthly trends reveal fluctuations, with notable peaks toward the end of the year.

Future-State Strategy

Incorporate predictive analytics to forecast future sales and demand patterns. Expand the dashboard to include profit margins and cost analysis for deeper insights. Integrate real-time data updates to support faster decision-making. Enhance user interactivity with drill-through and advanced filtering options.

Decision Framework Mapping

  • Supports strategic decisions by identifying high-performing categories and regions.
  • Enables tactical decisions through monthly and customer-level performance tracking.
  • Facilitates operational decisions by monitoring sales trends and fluctuations.
  • Aligns business goals with data-driven insights for improved efficiency.

Data Credibility

Data is structured and aggregated from reliable transactional sources. Consistent metrics and standardized calculations ensure accuracy across visuals. Filters and segmentation maintain transparency in data exploration. Regular validation and updates enhance trust and reliability of insights.
